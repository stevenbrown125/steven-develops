**Project:** Spudz Food Trucks Online Ordering Platform

**Overview:** This full-stack application addresses the unique challenge of providing online ordering for Spudz Food Trucks, which operate at variable locations and hours across Europe and now the USA. The dynamic nature of this business model required an innovative approach to ensure customer access to real-time information and seamless online ordering capabilities.

![Spudz Food Trucks Platform by Steven Brown - Desktop](/images/projects/spudz/spudz_desktop_screenshot.webp)

**Development Highlights:**

- **Responsive Design:** Developed with NextJS, the site is fully optimized for both mobile and desktop devices, ensuring a superior user experience regardless of the access point.
- **Dynamic Location and Hours:** Implemented a solution to accurately present the food trucks' varying locations and operating hours, enabling customers to find their nearest Spudz truck effortlessly.
- **Online Ordering System:** Integrated with Stripe API, the platform offers a secure and straightforward online ordering process, enhancing operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

![Spudz Food Trucks Platform - Mobile](/images/projects/spudz/spudz_mobile_screenshot.webp)

**Future Enhancements:**

- **Social Media Integration:** Plans include leveraging the Instagram API to deliver the latest posts directly to the homepage, further engaging the community and showcasing the food trucks' daily operations and specials.
