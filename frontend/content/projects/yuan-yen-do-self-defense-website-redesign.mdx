**Initial State:** The original Yuan Yen Do Self Defense website, built on Wordpress, suffered from outdated content, lack of mobile optimization, non-compliance with GDPR, and poor performance.

![Yuan Yen Do Website by Steven Brown - Redesigned](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/i80c8qr5/production/8631622e58404fe1540ee337b760d91ff238e011-1553x1168.png)

**Objectives:**

- **Optimization for All Devices:** Implement a responsive design to ensure an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices.
- **Performance Boost:** Enhance site speed and efficiency to improve user engagement and satisfaction.
- **Content Accuracy and Management:** Facilitate easy updates and management of content through Sanity Studio, ensuring information remains current and accurate.
- **GDPR Compliance:** Address data protection and privacy to meet European Union standards.

**Outcomes:**

- **Performance and Compliance:** The redesigned website not only achieved an average 20-point increase across all Lighthouse Audit categories but also ensured full GDPR compliance.
- **Mobile Optimization:** Delivered a seamless user experience on mobile devices through tailored design adjustments.
- **Content Management:** Enabled straightforward content updates via Sanity Studio, empowering non-technical staff to maintain the site's accuracy and relevance.

This project demonstrates a comprehensive approach to modernizing an outdated online presence, focusing on performance, compliance, and ease of content management.
